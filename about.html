The person behind this

Petar Ivanov

Senior Finance Analyst · Founder, The AI Enabled Finance

12 years in corporate finance.

Audit. Commodity trading. Manufacturing. Global health. Pharmaceutical.

I was doing fine.

Then AI came into the conversation.

Everyone started talking about it.
Nobody knew how to actually use it
inside a real corporate finance role.

Not safely.
Not compliantly.
Not in a way that survives a real deadline.

So six months ago I started testing it myself.

During actual month-end cycles.
Real CAPEX reviews.
Live board preparation.

Real data. Anonymised. Compliant. Under real pressure.

AI saved me 60 minutes on a four-hour close.

It also made a confident mistake
I nearly sent to my Finance Director.

That moment told me everything.

AI reasons well with data.
It cannot reason with context.
And in corporate finance — context is everything.

// The Mission

No BS. No guru. No vendor deck.

Just a finance professional documenting what actually works — and what does not — when you apply AI inside a real corporate finance role.

Safe. Compliant. Practical. Built for the finance professionals who want to stay ahead of the gap without putting their careers at risk.

That is what The AI Enabled Finance is.

What this platform is built on
01

Observation first. Every piece of content starts with something that actually happened — not something that could theoretically happen in a demo.

02

Safe by default. Every prompt, framework, and tool is built around GDPR, data privacy, and corporate governance constraints. Not as an afterthought. As the foundation.

03

Honest when it fails. AI gets things wrong. I document that too. The finance professionals who will use this well are the ones who understand both sides.

04

Peer to peer. Not above you. Not below you. I am a finance professional sharing what I found with other finance professionals. That is the only relationship this platform has.

05

Your judgment stays yours. The capital allocation decision. The re-phasing call. The stakeholder read. AI cannot make those. This platform never pretends otherwise.
