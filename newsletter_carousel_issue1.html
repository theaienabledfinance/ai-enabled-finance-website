Slide 05 — The Two Rules
The AI Enabled Finance What I Learned 05 / 06
Six months distilled into two rules

The only two things
that actually matter.

🛡️

Safe input first. Always.
Never paste live company data. Anonymise. Replace names. Convert absolutes. Then prompt.

✅

Your judgment is the last layer. Always.
AI accelerates the work. It cannot replace the understanding. Review everything. Correct what it cannot know.

// The Insight

The finance professionals who will use AI well are not the ones who use it most. They are the ones who use it most safely.
