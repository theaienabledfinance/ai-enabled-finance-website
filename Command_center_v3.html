#1Confession Hook"I was wrong about [specific thing]."

#3Specific Number"I used AI on [X] tasks this month. Here is the honest score."
#4Live Documentary"Day [X] of using AI to find my next finance job."
#5Challenge Hook"Your CV is not the problem. Your prompts are."
#6Observation Hook"I noticed something in my last interview nobody talks about."
#7Before/After"Without AI: 4 hours. With AI: 22 minutes. Same output."
📋 The 7 Content Rules — Never Break
1
Observation first. Always.
Never start with the lesson. Start with what you saw.
2
Specific over general. Always.
"60 minutes saved" not "significant time saving."
3
Failure is content. Always.
The mistake is the most valuable post you ever wrote.
4
One CTA. Always.
theaienabledfinance.com. Never two CTAs in one post.
5
Never teach. Always document.
"Here is what happened" not "here is what you should do."
6
Leave loops open. Always.
Every post ends with something unresolved.
7
Never AI-written language. Ever.
Your posts must sound exactly like you speak. No AI phrasing.
